Why is this necessary?

Facebook caches information about your URLs. When you update your site, Facebook may continue to show old information or "Page not found" errors until you manually refresh the cache.

Common Issues
  • Page not found: Facebook is trying to access a URL that doesn't exist or has been cached incorrectly
  • Wrong image: Facebook is showing an old or incorrect image for your link
  • Missing/wrong title or description: Facebook is using old metadata
How to fix:
  1. Use the tool above to generate a Facebook Debugger link for your URL
  2. Open the link and click "Scrape Again" on Facebook's page
  3. You may need to click "Scrape Again" 2-3 times for the changes to take effect
  4. Verify that your page information looks correct in the preview
