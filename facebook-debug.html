Facebook Sharing Debugger

If you're having trouble with how your links appear when shared on Facebook, follow these steps:

  1. Visit the Facebook Sharing Debugger Tool

    Go to https://developers.facebook.com/tools/debug/

  2. Enter the URL of your article

    Copy and paste the complete URL of the page you want to share

  3. Click "Debug"

    Facebook will analyze your page and show you what information it can extract

  4. Click "Scrape Again"

    This forces Facebook to clear its cache and fetch the latest version of your page

    You may need to click this button 2-3 times for the changes to take effect

  5. Check the preview

    Make sure your title, description, and image appear correctly in the preview

Why is this necessary?

Facebook caches information about your links. When you update your website, Facebook may continue showing old information until you manually refresh the cache using their debugger tool.

Common Issues

  • Missing Image: Make sure your article has a valid image specified in the front matter
  • Wrong Title/Description: Check the meta tags in the page's HTML
  • Page Not Found: Ensure the URL is valid and the page is publicly accessible
